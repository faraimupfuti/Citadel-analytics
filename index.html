The problem

Fraud doesn't wait for
your next rule update.

Static, rules-based fraud engines catch what they were built to catch — and nothing else. Fraud rings adapt in days; rule changes take months to write, test, and approve. The result is two costs at once: fraud that slips through, and legitimate customers who get declined, delayed, or flagged for a claim that was never suspicious to begin with.

Citadel replaces static thresholds with models that learn your institution's own patterns of normal and abnormal behavior — and keep learning as fraud tactics change.

How it works

One pipeline, from raw event to resolved case.

01

Ingest

Connect transaction, account-activity, and claims data through streaming or batch pipelines — no schema rewrite required.

02

Model

Ensemble ML models trained on your institution's historical patterns, continuously retrained as fraud tactics evolve.

03

Score

Every event receives a real-time risk score and a plain-language reason code your team can act on immediately.

04

Act

Route high-risk events to review, auto-hold, or block — inside the case management workflow your team already uses.
